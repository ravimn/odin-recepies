Biriyani

Description

Super Delicious Chicken Biryani.

Fuel up your plate with the most authentic and flavourful masala.

Ingredients

  1. 1 kg Chicken
  2. 1 kg Biriyani Rice
  3. 50 gms Priyom biryani masala powder
  4. 500 gms Onion
  5. 1/2 spoon Garlic powder
  6. 2 Ginger
  7. 8 - 10 Green Chillies
  8. 3- 6 Tomatos
  9. Coriander leaf, Curry leaf & Mint leaf as required
  10. Cashew Nut & Kaskas paste as required
  11. Salt to taste
  12. 1 cup Ghee

Steps

  1. Cut and clean chicken, grind ingredients 5, 6 and 7 in to paste. Slice 8, 9 and keep aside. Crush cashew and kaskas in to paste.
  2. Fry onions in ghee. Add the paste the sliced ingredients. Add Priyom biriyani masala( best biryani masala ) ,chicken pieces and cook.
  3. Wash and soak rice for 15 minutes. Fry it in ghee till dry. Add required water and boil. Mix the rice and chicken and pure ghee on in.
  4. Close the lid and bake for 30 minutes placing fire brands over and under vessel then serve hot.
